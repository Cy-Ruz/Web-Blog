Discover the unforgettable journey of learning and exploration in our BSCS Tour 2025 experience.

Three-Day Company Visit

Explore our immersive 3-day tour across top tech companies and institutions, giving students hands-on exposure to the real-world tech landscape and career opportunities.

Foundever PH

We were given a comprehensive orientation and facility tour, guided by the operations and IT teams. The session also featured an industry briefing from the IT and Business Process Association of the Philippines (IBPAP), highlighting trends and opportunities in the IT-BPM sector.

Google Philippines

A session led by Atty. Yves Gonzalez covered Google’s mission, innovations in artificial intelligence, digital inclusion initiatives, and public policy efforts. We also toured Google’s creative and collaborative office spaces.

Teleperformance Philippines

We explored the company’s Innovation and Solutions Hub and participated in HR-led sessions, mock interviews, and discussions centered on inclusivity, digital innovation, and the evolving workforce landscape.

Kollab

The visit featured talks by Kollab’s senior engineers and designers covering applied AI, UI/UX design, and quality assurance. It concluded with an engaging panel discussion and networking session with Kollab’s tech professionals.

Asian Development Bank (ADB) & CXC

In coordination with CXC, the visit included a tour of the ADB Library and History Gallery, a fireside chat with ADB’s IT directors, and a presentation of the students’ fintech project RePay. The program highlighted ADB’s digital transformation efforts and CXC’s global workforce solutions.

MicroSourcing

CEO Heidee Enriquez opened the session with a leadership talk on digital careers and adaptability. The visit featured a facility tour of their modern office setup and concluded with a LinkedIn Workshop focused on building a professional digital presence.

Reflection

The three-day industry immersion was a transformative experience that broadened my understanding of the tech industry. Engaging with professionals and touring top companies deepened my appreciation for the role of both technical and soft skills in today’s workplace. It inspired me to continuously improve, build a strong digital portfolio, and pursue a career that combines innovation with meaningful impact. This experience reminded me that real growth happens beyond the classroom, through exposure, reflection, and the willingness to adapt.
